Journey Through Space: Key Milestones

Explore the fascinating history of human space exploration, from the first steps into space to modern achievements in interplanetary travel.

The Space Race Begins

The 1950s and 60s marked the beginning of the space age, with the Soviet Union and United States competing to achieve major space milestones.

Moon Landing Achievement

On July 20, 1969, humans first set foot on another celestial body when Apollo 11 landed on the Moon.

Modern Space Exploration

Today, space exploration continues with international cooperation, private companies, and ambitious plans for Mars colonization.
